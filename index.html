What is GhostWire?

GhostWire publishes deep-dive investigations into state-sponsored cyber operations — written with full technical depth, but told as narrative. Each issue covers one threat actor: who they are, what they built, what they destroyed, and why it matters. Think Wired meets a declassified intelligence briefing. Independent research. No affiliations. No ads.

About GhostWire

GhostWire is an independent threat intelligence publication focused on original research into cyber operations, threat actor infrastructure, and attribution problems. Every investigation is evidence-first, confidence-calibrated, and told as narrative.

No advertising. No sponsored content. No affiliations.
